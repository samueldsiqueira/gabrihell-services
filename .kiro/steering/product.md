# Product Overview

**Gabrihell Services** is a professional landing page for Tibia hunting services. The website offers various gaming services including XP hunting, bestiary completion, access quests, and tasks for the MMORPG Tibia.

## Key Features
- Professional hunt services for Tibia players
- Service portfolio showcasing XP hunting, bestiary, access quests, and tasks
- Contact integration with WhatsApp, Discord, and Instagram
- Tibia-themed visual design with authentic game assets
- Portuguese language interface (pt-BR)

## Target Audience
- Tibia players seeking professional hunting services
- Players looking to level up characters efficiently
- Players needing help with bestiary completion or access quests

## Business Model
Service-based business offering professional gaming assistance for Tibia players, with direct contact through social platforms for booking and communication.
